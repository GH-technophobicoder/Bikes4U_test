About Us

Bikes4U was founded in 2022 to cater to the growing demand for cycling among busy adults who
have limited time to scout for a pre-assembled bike that suits their needs. At Bikes4U, we have a selection
of bicycles ranging from road bikes and foldable bikes to rough-and-ready mountain bikes
and the latest electric bikes. All our bikes are pre-assembled and tested by our certified
maintenance and repair team, and we offer bespoke service for any additional alterations.
